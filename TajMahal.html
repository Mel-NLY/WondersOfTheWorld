History & Information on Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal is one of the 7 Wonders in the World, it has been around for more than 300 years! Still standing tall and strong. The carvings and architecture make Taj Mahal look glorious and prestigious. Attracting millions of tourists annually. The Taj Mahal also constantly has subtle changes to it colour due to the sunlight shining on its marvellous white marbles.

Other then just Taj Mahal, nearby there is the Agra Fort, which is about 30 minutes walk away. You will have respresentitives and drivers assigned to you for the entire tour.

We aim to make your trip here enjoyable and fulfilling. We can cater to your needs and customise the tour for you if you wish to do so. Come and experience an engaging and fun-filled tour with us.

Taj Mahal Entry Ticket - 750 Indian Rupee (S$15.54)/Per pax

Opening Hours - Mondays to Thursdays, Saturdays & Sundays, From Sunrise to Sunset

Things to take note of
- Food and Drinks are not allowed inside
- Be mindful of your oufit! Even though there isn't a dresscode, it is still a religious infrastructure that deserves respect!

  • Fun Fact #1

    It was built as the final resting place for Mumtaz Mahal,
    the third wife of Mughal emperor Shah Jahan.

  • Fun Fact #2

    The Taj Mahal was built by a whopping 22,000 laborers,
    painters, stonecutters, embroidery artists.

  • Fun Fact #3

    Its intricate work of art and
    architectural genius took 17 years to complete.

  • Fun Fact #4

    Over 200,000 tourists from overseas and as many
    as 2 to 4 million people visit the Taj Mahal every year.

  • Fun Fact #5

    The palace was designed so that everything
    would fall away from the tomb in the event of a collapse.
